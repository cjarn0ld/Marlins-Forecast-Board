How It Works

1. Make Predictions

Submit probability-based forecasts (0-1) for various baseball questions. Higher difficulty questions offer more ranking impact.

2. Track Results

Once outcomes are determined, your predictions are scored using the Brier Score system for accuracy.

3. Compete & Learn

Compare your performance on the leaderboard and improve your forecasting skills over time.

Scoring System

Brier Score Explained

Formula: Brier = (probability - actual)², lower is better

Accuracy: 1 - Brier, higher is better

Example:

  • Your prediction: 0.7 (70% chance)
  • Actual outcome: 1 (happened)
  • Brier Score: (0.7 - 1)² = 0.09
  • Accuracy: 1 - 0.09 = 0.91 or 91%
